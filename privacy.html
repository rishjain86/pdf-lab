At Amazing PDF (accessible from amazingpdf.in), one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Amazing PDF and how we use it.

1. Local File Processing

Your privacy and document security are our utmost priority. Amazing PDF is designed to process the vast majority of your files locally in your web browser. This means that your documents are not uploaded to our servers, keeping your sensitive data entirely on your device.

2. Log Files

Amazing PDF follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. All hosting companies do this as part of hosting services' analytics. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Google is one of a third-party vendor on our site. It also uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to our site and other sites on the internet. However, visitors may choose to decline the use of DART cookies by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy at the following URL: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4. Third-Party Ad Networks

Third-party ad servers or ad networks uses technologies like cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons that are used in their respective advertisements and links that appear on Amazing PDF, which are sent directly to users' browser. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. These technologies are used to measure the effectiveness of their advertising campaigns and/or to personalize the advertising content that you see on websites that you visit.
